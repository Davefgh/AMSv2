FILTER BEHAVIOR:

Today Filter:
  - Auto-detects current day
  - Shows today's schedules
  - Default selection
  - Updates if app used across midnight

Day Filters:
  - Shows schedules for selected day
  - Maintains selection until changed
  - Works with all days (Mon-Sat)

Empty State:
  - Shows when no schedules for day
  - Displays friendly message
  - Allows day selection change
